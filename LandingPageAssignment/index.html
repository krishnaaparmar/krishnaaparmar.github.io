About Application

Shovl is a C2C snow clearing services based android application.

Shovl mainly focuses on the people who are busy working professionals, senior citizens or anyone who is not able to clear/shovel the snow after a heavy snow deposit due to snowstorm/snowfall.

This application allows user to hire the shoveler to clear the snow of their property and any shoveler who is looking for snow clearing work, it will help them to find potential cutsomer.
